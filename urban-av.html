Breathing Life into the Letters

Logo Design Concepts for Urban AV Systems

Urban AV Systems Inc. is a company that does professional audio/visual system installs. I was asked to come up with the concepts for their logo.

Missions

The most crucial part of the logo is to reflect the company's story and missions. The three qualities considered important for Urban AV Systems Inc. were urban, trustworthiness and quality-assurance.

The Logo

Here is the final logo made taking into account the company's values:

This icon is shaped like a diamond, symbolizing that the company assures high-quality results. It is also the alphabets "A" and "V" attached together while at the same time is just the alphabet "A" with the shadow under it. This means that the "A" (audio) and the "V" (video) always belong with each other.

Conclusion

Unfortunately, this concept was not chosen for final use. From interviewing the company and sketching designs to making high-fidelity logos, I've taken extensive approach during this process, and have learned that not only clear communication but also the strong explanation for each concept is vital.
