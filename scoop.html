Scoping for the Scoop

UI/UX Design, branding, graphic and motion design for the Scoop app.

Scoop is a platform where businesses can display their dynamically priced goods or services and notify users with real-time deals. Users can browse through the deals and make purchases directly to skip the line and save mone. The project needed a logo, UI design and promotional materials for presentation and social media.

1. Logo

The team members at Scoop were ambitious to start with the food industry and eventually grow to include more such as beauty and fashion industries. I had to include Scoop's mission - to provide real-time platform for both businesses and consumers - without having it limited to one industry.

After creating numerous designs, the simple logo displayed above was chosen as the final logo. The text and the half-elipse combined make a shape of a scoop, which customers can use to find real-time deals. The logo animation is shown below.

2. Animation

Some promotional materials such as animation was needed to present what Scoop is about to stakeholders and businesses. After Effects was used to create this short and quirky animation.

3.UI Design

The UI for the Scoop app was revised and improved continuosly after it was published. Below is the latest UI design for the app, made with Figma.

Scoop is no longer up and running; however, it was a valuable experience to be able to design and create everything for this company.
