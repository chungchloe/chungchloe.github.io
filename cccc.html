CCCC

Redesigning the membership page experience

UX Audit, Figma

Problem: CCCC, Canadian Centre for Christian Charities, was looking to have their high-stakeholder Membership page. Differentiated from regular for-profit memberships, each of CCCC’s membership is catered to specific charities or individuals. Distinction was important, but also was the hierarchy. Not one was more important than the other.

Approach: I did a UX audit on their current page, and found both large and small improvements to be made. It wasn’t clear which membership was catered to which audience, and it wasn’t clear what are where the call-to-action was. The overall hierarchy needed to be rearranged, and the design of the each membership layout had to be updated.

Solution: In collaboration with Story Digital, a creative agency, I identified the main goal of this page - to inform what each membership provides, and to clarify who it is catered to. Based on the objective, I worked on designing a new layout for their page.

Result: Using the provided solution, CCCC redeveloped their Membership page, and is now able to inform the audience better than before.
