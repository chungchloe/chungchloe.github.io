Logo Design for Upon This Love

Logo made with Illustrator

Worked closely with Upon This Love, a brand representing a wedding coordinator, to define the brand and visual identity.

Logos

Wedding is an event filled with not only love, but also sophistication. To represent this, but not make the visual symbol blunt, cursive letters were used to make an abstract symbol, while an olive branch were added to show sophistication.
